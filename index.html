Etch-A-Sketch

To use the website, simply click and drag on the Etch-A-Sketch board to create your designs.

To adjust the size of the board, enter a value between 2-64 in the textbox within the tools section and hit the set size button.

There are a variety of tools at your disposal, including a colored brush, rainbow brush, and an eraser.

To use the color brush, click on the circular color-picker and choose any color. Use the dropper to select a previously used color that is on the board.

To clear the grid, press the clear board button.
